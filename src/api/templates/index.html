M-CHAT-R/F Screening Questions

Modified Checklist for Autism in Toddlers, Revised with Follow-Up (Standard 20 Questions)

Please answer all questions based on how your child usually behaves. If the behavior is rare (e.g., seen only once or twice), answer as if the child does not do it.
1 If you point at something across the room, does your child look at it?
2 Have you ever wondered if your child might be deaf?
3 Does your child play pretend or make-believe? (e.g., feed a doll, talk on toy phone)
4 Does your child like climbing on things? (e.g., furniture, playground equipment, stairs)
5 Does your child make unusual finger movements near their eyes?
6 Does your child point with one finger to ask for something or to get help?
7 Does your child point with one finger to show you something interesting?
8 Is your child interested in other children? (watches, approaches, plays with)
9 Does your child show you things by bringing them to you or holding them up?
10 Does your child respond when you call his or her name?
11 When you smile at your child, does he or she smile back at you?
12 Does your child get upset by everyday sounds? (e.g., vacuum, loud music)
13 Does your child walk?
14 Does your child look you in the eye when you are talking, playing, or dressing?
15 Does your child try to copy what you do? (e.g., wave bye-bye, clap, make sounds)
16 If you turn your head to look at something, does your child look around to see what you are looking at?
17 Does your child try to get you to watch him or her? ("Look at me!")
18 Does your child understand when you tell him or her to do something?
19 If something new happens, does your child look at your face to see how you feel?
20 Does your child like movement activities? (being swung, bounced on knee)
M-CHAT-R Scoring Guide
Low Risk (0-2): If screen negative, no further action required unless surveillance indicates ASD risk.
Medium Risk (3-7): Administer Follow-Up (M-CHAT-R/F) to get additional information.
High Risk (8-20): Bypass Follow-Up and refer immediately for diagnostic evaluation.
