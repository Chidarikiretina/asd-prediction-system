ASD Early Screening System

A machine learning-powered tool designed to support healthcare providers in Zimbabwe for early detection of Autism Spectrum Disorder in children aged 18-36 months. Our system combines validated screening protocols with advanced predictive analytics to help identify children who may benefit from early intervention services.

Our Mission

To improve early detection of autism spectrum disorder in Zimbabwe through accessible, technology-driven screening tools that empower healthcare workers at all levels.

Target Users

Designed for pediatricians, nurses, and community health workers in primary healthcare settings, clinics, and hospitals throughout Zimbabwe.

Evidence-Based

Built on internationally validated screening tools including M-CHAT-R and behavioral indicators recognized by clinical research.

Key Features

Quick 5-minute screening assessment with comprehensive behavioral checklist
Machine learning model trained on validated ASD screening data
Instant risk assessment with probability scores and recommendations
Secure record keeping with screening history and patient tracking
Professional printable reports for referrals and documentation
Dashboard analytics with screening trends and statistics
Designed for low-bandwidth environments and mobile devices
Multi-user support with role-based access control

Important Disclaimer: This screening tool is designed to assist healthcare professionals in identifying children who may benefit from further evaluation. It is NOT a diagnostic tool. Results should be interpreted by qualified healthcare professionals and should be considered alongside comprehensive clinical evaluation. A positive screening result indicates a need for further assessment, not a confirmed diagnosis of Autism Spectrum Disorder.
